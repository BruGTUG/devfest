Mostafa Akbari (Germany)

mobile, Android, soical business, basketball, Kölsch, 2.0, e-Learning

Mostafa Mostafa has a diploma in CS with minor in psychology. He worked as a researcher and gained StartUp experience as CTO at the Interpad Project. Besides he consulted some startUps and cooperations until now in topics of mobile, software product development. In 2006 he ran his first AR project at RWTH Aachen. He has been working on AR with Simon Heinen till 2009. In 2012 they started bitstars.

Stefan Hoth (Germany)

Professional developer, community manager and geek living in Berlin, Germany.

Stefan is a freelance developer interested in a lot of technologies. Recently he finished a few Android projects but before that he did projects in Java, PHP, Javascript. In his sparetime he likes to share curiosity for new frameworks, languages and best practices by co-organizing the GDG Berlin, Berlin Hack and Tell and OpenTechSchool Berlinin.

Friedger Müffke

Organizer of GTUG Brussels, Android developer at Novoda

Friedger is organizer of several Android and developer conferences and events, including Droidcon, Global Android Dev Camp and V Hack Android. He is a passionate open source developer for Android.

Pieter-Jan Vandormael

Organizer of GTUG Brussels

Before DevFest, Pieter-Jan brought extended hackathon formats, such as Global Android Dev Camp and V Hack Android, to West-European soil. With his Java background, he has been on the Android track from the beginning, developing apps in his spare time. And having a keen interest in extending the open source platform at its base, through patching, sharing common tools and building blocks, building and engaging communities, he tries to steer where this train is going as well.
