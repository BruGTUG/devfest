Pieter-Jan Vandormael

Organizer of GTUG Brussels

Before DevFest, Pieter-Jan brought extended hackathon formats, such as Global Android Dev Camp and V Hack Android, to West-European soil. With his Java background, he has been on the Android track from the beginning, developing apps in his spare time. And having a keen interest in extending the open source platform at its base, through patching, sharing common tools and building blocks, building and engaging communities, he tries to steer where this train is going as well.
